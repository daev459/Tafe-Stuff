Our History

TipTop Shoes began on the sunny Gold Coast in 1986, when local sports enthusiast Mark Donovan started hand-crafting supporting running shoes for friends in his surg and fitness community. Australia's outdoor lifestyle - long coastal walks, weekend hikes, and early-morning runs - inspired Donovan to design footwear that was lightweight, stablising, and built to withstand real everyday movement.

What started as a small garage workshop quickly gained traction among athletes and active families alike. By the mid-90s, TipTop Shoes had developed their first breakthrough: the FlexStep midsole, engineered to provide soft landings and powerful toe-off energy. From there, the brand expanded into walking shoes, cross-training sneakers, and slip-resistant work footwear.

Today, TipTop Shoes remains proudly Australian-owned, continuing to design footwear suited for our diverse terrain - from coastal tracks to city pavements. The mission has never changed: create high-performance shoes that help Australians stay active, comfortable, and confident on every step of the journey.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower our customers to move more - whether that's running, walking, training, or simply staying active in everyday life. We aim to lead the industry with footwear that feels lightweight, supportive, and performance-driven, without compromising style or sustainablity.

At TipTop Shoes, we are committed to:

• advancing active footwear technology

• reducing our environmental footprint

• creating shoes that suit real Aussie Conditions

• supporting healthy, movement-filled lifestyles.

We believe that when your shoes feel good, your whole day feels better. Every pair we make is designed to help people move freely, confidently, and in comfort - from the gym to the track, and everywhere in between.

About Our Team

Our team is made up of designers, podiatry consultants, material engineers, and everyday athletes who share a passion for movement. Based across Sydney, Brisbane, and Melbourne, wee test every design in real life Aussie environments -hot weather, long distances, uneven terrain, and fast-paced daily life.

From biomechanical experts who refine our arch support systems, to textile specialists developing breathable mesh uppers, every member of the TipTop Shoes team contributes to creating footwear that performs under pressure. We regularly partner with local running clubs, physiotherapists, personal trainers, and community sports groups to ensure our shoes don't just look good - they genuinely improve the way people move!

At TipTop Shoes, we believe the best innovations come from listening. Customer feedback directly shapes our new collections, helping us create footwear that supports our customers of all activity levels.
